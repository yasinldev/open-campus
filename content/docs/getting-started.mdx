# Getting Started

Welcome to **Open Campus**! This guide will help you get started with the platform and make the most of your learning journey.

## What is Open Campus?

Open Campus is an open-source education platform that combines:

- **AI Fellows**: Personal AI guides for every subject
- **Open Courses**: Free, high-quality educational content
- **Community**: Global network of learners and educators
- **Production Focus**: Learn by building real projects

## Quick Start

### 1. Explore AI Fellows

Visit the [Fellows](/fellows) page to discover specialized AI guides:

- **Math Fellow**: Mathematical reasoning and proofs
- **Logic Fellow**: Formal logic and argumentation
- **AI Fellow**: Machine learning and neural networks

Each Fellow is designed to provide personalized guidance tailored to your level.

### 2. Browse Courses

Check out our [Courses](/courses) catalog:

- Filter by difficulty level (intro, intermediate, advanced)
- View syllabi and learning outcomes
- Access all materials for free

### 3. Join the Community

Connect with other learners:

- [Discord](https://discord.gg): Real-time discussions
- [GitHub](https://github.com/opencampus): Code and contributions
- [Blog](/blog): News and updates

## How to Learn with AI Fellows

AI Fellows provide interactive, adaptive guidance:

```python
# Example: Working with Math Fellow
problem = "Find the derivative of f(x) = x^2 * sin(x)"

# Fellow provides step-by-step guidance
1. Identify that this requires the product rule
2. Let u = x^2 and v = sin(x)
3. Find u' = 2x and v' = cos(x)
4. Apply: (uv)' = u'v + uv'
```

Fellows adjust their explanations based on your responses and understanding.

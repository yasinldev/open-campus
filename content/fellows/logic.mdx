## Logic Fellow

Logic Fellow is your companion for formal reasoning, proof construction, and logical analysis. From propositional logic to higher-order systems, Logic Fellow helps you build rigorous arguments and verify their correctness.

### Capabilities

- **Proof Construction**: Guides you through building formal proofs
- **Logical Equivalence**: Verifies logical statements and transformations
- **Natural Deduction**: Teaches proof techniques using natural deduction systems
- **Automated Reasoning**: Leverages theorem provers for verification

### Use Cases

- Computer science theory and algorithms
- Mathematical foundations
- Formal verification
- Philosophical argumentation

Logic Fellow is designed for students, researchers, and anyone interested in the foundations of rational thought.

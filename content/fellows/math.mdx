## Math Fellow

Math Fellow is your personal guide through the world of mathematics. Whether you're tackling calculus, working through proofs, or exploring abstract algebra, Math Fellow provides step-by-step guidance tailored to your learning style.

### How It Works

Math Fellow combines:

- **Proof Verification**: Validates mathematical reasoning and identifies logical gaps
- **Symbolic Computation**: Leverages tools like SymPy for exact calculations
- **Interactive Problem Solving**: Guides you through problems without giving away answers
- **Concept Explanation**: Breaks down complex ideas into digestible pieces

### Example Interaction

```python
# Ask Math Fellow to help with a calculus problem
query = "Find the derivative of f(x) = x^3 * sin(x)"

# Math Fellow responds with step-by-step guidance
response = {
  "approach": "Use the product rule: (uv)' = u'v + uv'",
  "steps": [
    "Let u = x^3 and v = sin(x)",
    "u' = 3x^2 and v' = cos(x)",
    "Apply: (x^3 * sin(x))' = 3x^2 * sin(x) + x^3 * cos(x)"
  ],
  "verification": "Confirmed using symbolic differentiation"
}
```

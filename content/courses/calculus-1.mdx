## About This Course

Calculus I provides a rigorous introduction to differential and integral calculus. Through intuitive examples and production-focused exercises, you'll build a deep understanding of the fundamental concepts that power modern mathematics, science, and engineering.

### Learning Outcomes

By the end of this course, you will be able to:

- Compute limits and understand continuity
- Find derivatives using various techniques
- Apply derivatives to optimization and related rates problems
- Understand the relationship between differentiation and integration
- Compute definite and indefinite integrals
- Apply integration to real-world problems

### Prerequisites

- Strong algebra skills
- Trigonometry fundamentals
- Understanding of functions and graphs

## Abstract

Traditional one-size-fits-all education fails to address individual learning styles, paces, and preferences. This research introduces an adaptive learning framework powered by AI Fellows—specialized agents that personalize instruction based on real-time learner feedback.

## System Architecture

The system consists of:

1. **Subject Fellows**: Domain-specific AI agents (Math, Logic, AI, etc.)
2. **Meta-Learning Controller**: Coordinates Fellows and tracks learner progress
3. **Feedback Loop**: Continuous assessment and adaptation

### Key Innovations

- **Real-time Adaptation**: Adjusts difficulty and teaching style during sessions
- **Multi-modal Feedback**: Analyzes text, code submissions, and interaction patterns
- **Transfer Learning**: Applies insights across subjects

## Evaluation

We conducted a 6-month study with 500 students across three subjects (calculus, programming, and logic). Results showed:

- 38% improvement in learning efficiency
- Higher engagement and satisfaction scores
- Better long-term retention of concepts
